Compliance data: Mandatory compliance, regulators, and audit requirements live in 07-canada-schema.html.
Column definitions — 5 market dimensions

Primary hubs: Which of the 14 named Canadian hubs concentrate this industry. Format: Hub (T1), Hub (T2). When no hub applies: –

Tier: Employer concentration. T1 = 3+ major employers active. T2 = 1–2 major + SME cluster. T3 = SME-dominated, no employer >20%. T4 = Government-only (>80% of roles).

Demand: Directional signal for DevSecOps-adjacent roles. Hot = posting growth >15% YoY. Warm = 5–15% YoY. Flat = <5% change. Declining = postings falling.

Wage band (CA$): Annual salary for mid-level DevSecOps-adjacent roles (3–7 years experience). Toronto and Vancouver: +8–15% above national range.

Entry: Difficulty score 1–5 for first role. 1 = Very easy (high junior volume). 2 = Easy. 3 = Moderate. 4 = Hard (mostly senior or clearance). 5 = Very hard (<5% junior postings, clearance mandatory).

Sources: Robert Half Canada 2026 · Job Bank Canada (NOC 21220, 21231) · CBRE Tech Talent Canada 2025 · Canadian Cybersecurity Network. Last verified: May 2026.

Provincial Nominee Programs (PNP) — 2026

Total PNP nominations in 2026: 91,500 — up from 55,000 in 2025.
Why: Largest single-year PNP expansion in program history. Federal immigration targets increased.
Coverage: 4 provinces with active tech-sector streams shown — BC, AB, ON, SK · remaining 9 provinces have no dedicated tech stream as of May 2026.
Source: canxglobal.com · gofarglobal.com. Last verified: May 2026.
Province Stream Processing Constraint
BC BC Tech Pilot 2–3 months 29 eligible tech occupations only. Draws held most Tuesdays. Expedited vs. regular BC PNP (4–6 months).
AB Accelerated Tech Pathway ~1 month Fastest processing in Canada as of May 2026. Bypasses standard provincial queue.
ON Employer Job Offer streams 4–6 months Express Entry-linked OINP streams suspended through 2025 and into 2026. Ontario employer job offer required first.
SK Priority sector nominations Varies Tech added as priority sector in 2026. 50% of SK nominations reserved for 7 priority sectors.
Source: immigcanada.com · libertyimmigration.ca · canxglobal.com. Last verified: May 2026.
